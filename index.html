Celebrate Diwali 2024: A Festival of Lights

Introduction Diwali

The Festival of Lights, is one of the most cherished celebrations in India. In 2024, this vibrant festival will be celebrated on November 1st. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil, making it a time for joy, reflection, and togetherness.

A Festival Celebrate

Traditions and Rituals

The festivities begin with cleaning and decorating homes, symbolizing the welcoming of Goddess Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth and prosperity. People adorn their houses with colorful rangoli, diyas (oil lamps), and fairy lights.
